Photography Tips for Manali

Golden Hours

Shoot during sunrise and sunset for the best lighting on mountains. Early morning offers clear skies and crisp views.

Equipment

Bring a sturdy tripod for low-light shots and a wide-angle lens for landscapes. Don't forget extra batteries!

Weather

Check weather conditions before heading out. Cloudy days can create dramatic mountain photographs.
